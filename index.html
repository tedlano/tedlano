About Me

You've found my creative space. I've always wanted to get better at web development and writing, so here's where I've set up shop. This is mostly a way for me to write down my own thoughts and opinions, and tailor them as if somebody else is reading. I figured that maybe some of the things that I write down in my personal journal could have some value to a broader audience. Journalling is one thing, blogging is a whole different beast. I may be a terrible writer now, but I don't stay terrible at things for long.

I just turned 26 and made the decision to leave my job to try something different for a while, just because I can. I could give a lot of excuses as to why I'm not in peak physical condition, or why I cannot speak Mandarin fluently, or why this website isn't the greatest personal site I've envisioned it to be. The real reason is that I let my surrounding environment determine my priorities, and I didn't have the discipline or energy to push back. Now when I wake up, I have free reign over what I'll be doing the entire day. I could choose to invest that time, or I choose to waste it. Only thing that can get in the way is my own discipline.

So here I am. I've set some priorities in my life and the only person that can stop me is myself. The purpose of this website is to track my progress, and hold myself accountable to them. I'll be heading to China in a few days to immerse myself in the language and culture in order to become better-suited as an aspiring international know-it-all. I don't think I'll have this sort of freedom from responsibility for much longer: no debt, no wife, no kids, no worries what-so-ever. It will only get more difficult to take risks as we get older, so there is no better time than right now to take some. Here's to a new, exiciting chapter of life. I hope you'll enjoy it as much as I do.
