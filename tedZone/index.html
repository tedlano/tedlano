Cool Internet Resources

Here are some of my favorite internet resources, especially because I feel productive when visiting them opposed to everything else on the internet. I am honestly ashamed of all the time I waste on other internet sites, but funny pictures of cats aren't going to laugh at themselves.

Category Name Description
Educational Videos Kurzgesagt Youtube Channel My favorite Youtube Channel by far. Their animations are crisp and simplistic, and the narratives are clear and effective. Burn a few minutes here and learn a thing or two about interesting things.
Educational Videos CrashCourse Youtube Channel Another great educational channel on Youtube. The videos are a bit longer, but they cram an amazing amount of information into them. Well produced, well researched, and well executed..
Blog Mark Manson Blog I'm actually afraid to link this because now it looks like I'm trying to rip this guy off, which is sort of true because I wish I were more like him. His blogs are well-written, well-informed, and incredibly interesting. He also has accomplishments to back up his words, whereas I am mostly just pontificating.
Blog Mark Cuban Blog I learned more about Mark Cuban by watching the TV show "Shark Tank". I bought his ebook, which is really a collection of some of his blog entries, and read through it during a flight. What's not to love about a super sucessful entrepreneur who follows their instincts?
Educational Memrise.com This is my favorite language-learning resource. I currently use it to study Mandarin and Tagalog, but have also used it for different subjects in the past. I have even created my own courses: The Seven Chakras and Internet Mandarin.
